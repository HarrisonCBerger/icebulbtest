About Ice Bulb
Welcome to Ice Bulb, Southern California’s premier destination for custom ice creations. Servicing San Diego, Orange and Los Angeles counties, Ice Bulb is recognized as the leader in creating some of the world’s most phenomenal ice concepts.

For over 15 years, we’ve been the leaders in transforming frozen water into extraordinary experiences. Whether you’re looking for the perfect crystal-clear cubes to elevate your cocktail presentation, a stunning ice sculpture that’s the talk of the event, or an intricate ice luge that adds a dash of excitement, Ice Bulb is your go-to partner.

Full Service
Our team of skilled artisans and technicians combines innovation with craftsmanship to deliver ice creations that are not only visually striking but also structurally sound. We’re proud to have served countless events, from intimate gatherings to large corporate functions, providing high-quality custom ice that meets the highest standards.

Event Packages
Whether you’re hosting a wedding, corporate event, or any celebration that requires a touch of frozen elegance, Ice Bulb is here to ensure your event is nothing short of spectacular.  Ice Bulb is unmatched in its ability to produce large events.
